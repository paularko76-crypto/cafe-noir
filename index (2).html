Our Story

Born from a
Passion for
the Perfect Cup

Café Noir began with a single dream: to create a space where time slows down and every cup carries the story of the hands that grew and roasted the beans. Nestled in the heart of New Delhi, we opened our doors in 2019 — and haven't stopped brewing since.

We work directly with single-origin farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Coorg — ensuring every sip is traceable, ethical, and extraordinary. Our baristas are trained at world-class academies so your experience is always consistent, always exceptional.

"We don't just serve coffee — we curate mornings, afternoons, and quiet late evenings."

— Rohan Mehta, Founder
